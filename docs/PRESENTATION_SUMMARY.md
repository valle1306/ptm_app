## Pro Tips

1. **Print speaker notes** - having physical copy is confidence-building
2. **Use a pointer** - helps audience follow along (don't gesture at screen)
3. **Pacing** - go slower than feels natural (you're more rushed than you think)
4. **Eye contact** - look at audience, not slides
5. **Pause for effect** - let important ideas sink in
6. **Demo practice** - do it at least 3 times before presenting
7. **Have backup** - screenshot key demo moments
8. **Know your material** - read speaker notes multiple times
9. **Enjoy it** - you've done great work, share your enthusiasm
10. **Q&A warmly** - "great question!" builds rapport
